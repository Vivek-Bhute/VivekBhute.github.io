First Year

Semester 1 & Semester 2

This year typically focuses on building a strong Foundations and Basic Engineering Sciences concepts. The goal is to equip students with the necessary theoretical knowledge.
It includes "Basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills" with some internal assesment subjects for Engineering drawing and basic workshop skills.

Second Year

Semester 3 & Semester 4

This year marks the transition to more core Engineering Subjects and Branch-Specific Introduction. Students start to delve deeper into their chosen field.
It Introduces students to tha Programming world with Data structures and algorithms, digital electronics and computer organization. Also includes Introduction to networks and web development.

Third Year

Semester 5 & Semester 6

This final year is heavily focused on the student's Specialization and Advanced Practical Skills in their chosen branch of engineering. It often includes industrial visits or internships to provide real-world exposure.
Advanced programming, software engineering, and network administration. Project work and elective subjects.
