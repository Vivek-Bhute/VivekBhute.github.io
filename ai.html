Information :

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) Diploma is a 3-year program designed to provide students with practical skills and knowledge in machine learning, data analysis, and AI application development. It prepares students for entry-level positions in the rapidly growing AI and data science fields. Emphasis is placed on hands-on experience, coding projects, and data analysis.


Knowledge you get:

An Artificial Intelligence (AI) Diploma equips you with a diverse set of skills essential for the AI and data science industry. You will be introduced to several core concepts and technologies, including

  • Programming in Python
  • Machine Learning Algorithms
  • Data Analysis
  • Database Management
  • Data Visualization
  • Deep Learning (Introduction)
  • Natural Language Processing (Introduction)
  • AI Ethics

which are fundamental for software development. The curriculum also covers other programming languages & web development, where you will learn following languages:

  • C
  • C++
  • Python
  • Java
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript



Job/Career Opportunities:

Upon completing an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Diploma, you will have a range of career opportunities in the AI and data science industries.

    You can get opportunities as :

  • Data Annotation Specialist
  • AI/ML Assistant
  • Junior Data Analyst
  • AI Application Tester
  • Data Entry Operator (with AI focus)

Furthermore, Pimpri-Chinchwad, being a significant industrial hub in Maharashtra with increasing adoption of AI in manufacturing and IT, offers numerous opportunities in AI-related roles for diploma holders.


An Artificial Intelligence (AI) Diploma provides a solid foundation for a career in the AI and data science industry. It equips students with practical skills and knowledge that are in demand by employers. With continuous learning and skill development, graduates can advance their careers and pursue higher education. In a tech-driven city like Pimpri-Chinchwad, there are growing opportunities for AI diploma holders to succeed.

First Year

Semester 1 & Semester 2

This year typically focuses on building a strong foundation in basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills. The goal is to equip students with the necessary theoretical knowledge. It includes "Basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills" with some internal assessment subjects for Engineering drawing and basic workshop skills.

Second Year

Semester 3 & Semester 4

This year marks the transition to more core Automobile Engineering Subjects. Students start to delve deeper into their chosen field. It introduces students to the fundamentals of vehicle systems, including engine mechanisms, transmission systems, and chassis design. Also includes introduction to automotive materials and manufacturing processes.

Third Year

Semester 5 & Semester 6

This final year is heavily focused on the student's Specialization and Practical Application in their chosen branch of automobile engineering. It often includes industrial visits or internships to provide real-world exposure. Advanced topics like vehicle maintenance and repair, automotive electrical systems, and emission control. Project work and elective subjects are included.
