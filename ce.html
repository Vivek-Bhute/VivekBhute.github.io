Information :

The Civil Engineering Diploma is a 3-year program designed to provide students with practical skills and knowledge in construction materials, surveying, structural analysis, and construction management. It prepares students for entry-level positions in the construction and infrastructure development industries. Emphasis is placed on hands-on experience, site visits, and project work.


Knowledge you get:

A Civil Engineering Diploma equips you with a diverse set of skills essential for the civil engineering industry. You will be introduced to several core concepts and technologies, including:

  • Surveying
  • Building Materials
  • Construction Technology
  • Strength of Materials
  • Structural Analysis
  • Estimation and Costing
  • Construction Management
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering

along with some technical knowledge like Programming and Web page designing too.



Job/Career Opportunities:

Upon completing a Civil Engineering Diploma, you will have a range of career opportunities in the construction and infrastructure development industries.

    You can get opportunities as :

  • Site Supervisor
  • Junior Engineer
  • Surveyor
  • Draftsman
  • Quality Control Technician
  • Estimation Assistant

Furthermore, Pimpri-Chinchwad, being a significant industrial hub in Maharashtra with ongoing infrastructure projects, offers numerous opportunities in construction and related companies for civil engineering diploma holders.


A Civil Engineering Diploma provides a solid foundation for a career in the civil engineering industry. It equips students with practical skills and knowledge that are in demand by employers. With continuous learning and skill development, graduates can advance their careers and pursue higher education. In a developing city like Pimpri-Chinchwad, there are many opportunities for civil engineering diploma holders to succeed.

First Year

Semester 1 & Semester 2

This year typically focuses on building a strong foundation in basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills. The goal is to equip students with the necessary theoretical knowledge. It includes "Basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills" with some internal assessment subjects for Engineering drawing and basic workshop skills.

Second Year

Semester 3 & Semester 4

This year marks the transition to more core Civil Engineering Subjects. Students start to delve deeper into their chosen field. It introduces students to the fundamentals of surveying, building materials, and construction technology. Also includes introduction to strength of materials and basic structural analysis.

Third Year

Semester 5 & Semester 6

This final year is heavily focused on the student's Specialization and Practical Application in their chosen branch of civil engineering. It often includes site visits or internships to provide real-world exposure. Advanced topics like estimation and costing, construction management, transportation engineering, and environmental engineering. Project work and elective subjects are included.
