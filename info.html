About Institute:

Govt. Residential Women's Polytechnic, Yavatmal was established in 1994 with a primary aim to impart Technician Education exclusively to girls. Later, in 2018 the institute is renamed as ‘Govt. Polytechnic Yavatmal’ with the facility of co-education for both boys and girls. The Institute has started three new courses (Civil Engg, Mechanical Engg, Electrical Engg) from 2019 to impart diploma level technical education in diverse disciplines. The Institute provides quality manpower to the higher industries and service sectors.


LOCATION:
The Institute is located in Vidharbha region at Yavatmal city on Dhamangao Road, Yavatmal with the beautiful campus spread over 15.2 hector of land along with administrative, instructional building, girls hostel, trainee hostel, auditorium, recreation hall, playground and residential quarters. Apart from providing technical education the institute also offers services to various Govt. institutes and other private organizations. The institute is also instrumental in the field of the rural area development through Community Polytechnic Wing. The institute strives to achieve excellence through its modern equipment, physical resources, quality human resources and well-developed instructional resources.
