Information :

The Automobile Engineering Diploma is a 3-year program designed to provide students with practical skills and knowledge in vehicle design, manufacturing, maintenance, and repair. It prepares students for entry-level positions in the automotive and related industries. Emphasis is placed on hands-on experience, workshop practice, and project development.


Knowledge you get:

An Automobile Engineering Diploma equips you with a diverse set of skills essential for the automotive industry. You will be introduced to several core concepts and technologies, including :

  • Engine Mechanisms
  • Transmission Systems
  • Chassis Design
  • Automotive Materials
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Vehicle Maintenance and Repair
  • Automotive Electrical Systems
  • Emission Control



Job/Career Opportunities:

Upon completing an Automobile Engineering Diploma, you will have a range of career opportunities in the automotive and related industries.

    You can get opportunities as :

  • Automotive Technician
  • Service Advisor
  • Workshop Supervisor
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Automotive Designer (Junior Level)
  • Sales Engineer (Automotive)

Furthermore, Pimpri-Chinchwad, being a significant industrial hub in Maharashtra with a strong automotive presence, offers numerous opportunities in automotive manufacturing, service centers, and related companies for automobile engineering diploma holders.


An Automobile Engineering Diploma provides a solid foundation for a career in the automotive industry. It equips students with practical skills and knowledge that are in demand by employers. With continuous learning and skill development, graduates can advance their careers and pursue higher education. In a manufacturing-rich city like Pimpri-Chinchwad, there are many opportunities for automobile engineering diploma holders to succeed.

First Year

Semester 1 & Semester 2

This year typically focuses on building a strong foundation in basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills. The goal is to equip students with the necessary theoretical knowledge. It includes "Basic mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills" with some internal assessment subjects for Engineering drawing and basic workshop skills.

Second Year

Semester 3 & Semester 4

This year marks the transition to more core Automobile Engineering Subjects. Students start to delve deeper into their chosen field. It introduces students to the fundamentals of vehicle systems, including engine mechanisms, transmission systems, and chassis design. Also includes introduction to automotive materials and manufacturing processes.

Third Year

Semester 5 & Semester 6

This final year is heavily focused on the student's Specialization and Practical Application in their chosen branch of automobile engineering. It often includes industrial visits or internships to provide real-world exposure. Advanced topics like vehicle maintenance and repair, automotive electrical systems, and emission control. Project work and elective subjects are included.
